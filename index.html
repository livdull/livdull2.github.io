Olivia Tull is an artist, creative director, and filmmaker born and raised in Los Angeles, California. she is known for working in both digital and analog, as well as experimental films, music videos, re-working found footage and directing some narrative based projects, those with themes consisting of ritual motifs, existence, identity, and sound work. ​BFA from the California Institute of the Arts.
